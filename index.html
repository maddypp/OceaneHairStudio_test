About

Welcome to Océane Hair Studio!

I have over 15 years of experience in the hair industry, and I was trained in France before moving to Australia in 2017.

I created this hair studio with a true passion about health, sustainability, and the environment. I wanted to create a space that feels different from any other salon—a place that’s not just about getting your hair done, but about having a peaceful, calming experience.

This is your chance to escape the rush of everyday life and enjoy the serenity of a space surrounded by nature, with a lovely garden view to help you relax.

As a one-on-one salon, I focus on giving you my full attention and care. You’ll never feel rushed or overlooked. Instead, I’ll work with you to create a look that highlights your natural beauty and suits your unique style.

I only use organic, eco-friendly hair products, because I believe in taking care of both your hair and your health.

Come on in, and experience the calm of nature, the warmth of personalized service, and the beauty of organic hair care.
